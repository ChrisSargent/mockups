About Us

Panic Transport (Contracts) Limited is a privately owned transport company that has been trading since 1990. From that point forward it has enjoyed steady managed growth and has developed into a 6.5 million company with over 60 vehicles and 45 trailers. Over the years the name Panic has become synonymous with excellent service and customer care. Panic Transport provides a genuine one-stop shop solution. This is only possible due to a dedicated hardworking team of drivers and staff striving to achieve the same goal.
